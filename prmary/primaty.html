How To Teach in Primary

Prepare Spiritually

Begin your preparation by studying the scriptures and lesson materials prayerfully. Seek guidance from the Spirit about what your class needs.

Know the Children

Learn about the children you teach - their interests, learning styles, and needs. Tailor your lessons to help each child feel valued.

Create Engaging Lessons

Use a variety of teaching methods including stories, songs, object lessons, and activities to engage different learning styles.

Invite Participation

Encourage children to share their thoughts and experiences. Ask open-ended questions and create opportunities for discussion.

Bear Testimony

Share your testimony of gospel truths frequently. Help children recognize and share their own growing testimonies.
